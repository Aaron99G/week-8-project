Push Pull Legs or PPL is a workout split is where you split the muscle groups that you will workout by those that you use to push/pull and legs on their own

This one is really good to look more asthetic, but it can be adjusted to focus a bit more on strength

Upper Lower Split is a workout split where you focus on your upper body muscle one day, and lower body the next

Not the best split, but it is good for those that can not workout too frequently

Power Hypertrophy Upper Lower or PHUL is a workout split in which you will work out for power/strength and then hypertrophy/bigger muscles

Almost identicle to the Upper Lower, split exept that you also train for strength.
